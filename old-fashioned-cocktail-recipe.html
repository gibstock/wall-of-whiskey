Old Fashioned

If you don't know what to order at a bar, order an old fashioned.

They're all unique, and almost always delicious!

There's no "right" or "wrong" way, but here's how I like em:

    Ingredients
  • 2 oz Whiskey of your choice
    (I prefer a high rye)
  • .25 oz simple syrup (1:1)
  • 2 strong dashes of
    Angostura Bitters
  • Orange peel for garnish
  • Brandied Cherry for garnish
    Tools
  • Mixing Glass
  • Bar Spoon
  • Julep Strainer
  • Double Old Fashioned Glass
  • Jigger
  • Large Ice Cube (optional)
    Method
  1. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass.
  2. Fill with ice.
  3. Stir consistently for a good 40 rotations.
  4. Pour into a chilled double old fashioned glass over a big cube.
  5. Express the oil from the orange peel into the glass and rub the peel along the full rim of the glass and lay it on the cube.
  6. Rest the cherry on top of the orange peel.
    7. Enjoy!
