Hot Toddy

Hot Toddy's are a magical thing.

I cannot tell you how many sick people I've cured with one of my Toddy's.

I can 100% guaranetee that these will make you feel better! And here's how I make mine:

    Ingredients
  • 1.5 oz Whiskey of your choice
  • .75 oz of freshly squeesed
    lemon juice
  • .50 oz honey syrup (1:1)
  • Dash of Angostura Bitters
  • Clove studded lemon peel for garnish
  • Hot water
    • suggested additions
  • .50 oz Becharovka
  • .25 oz Ginger Syrup
  • Dash of Lemon Bitters
    Tools
  • Cocktail Shaker /w Cheater Tin
  • Bar Spoon
  • Jigger
  • Footed glass with handle
    Method
  1. Start by filling the footed glass with hot water to warm the glass.
  2. Then fill the smaller "cheater" tin with a quarter of the way with hot water.
  3. Next, place the bigger shaker tin into the smaller tin so its floating on the hot water.
    Here you're making a little 'stove'.
  4. Now, fill the bigger tin with the rest of the ingredients stirring with the bar spoon.
  5. Stir for about a minute to heat the ingredients and get it all nice and homogenous.
  6. Empty the now-warmed footed glass.
  7. Carefully, (IT'S HOT!) pour the concoction into the footed glass.
  8. Express the oil from the lemon peel into the glass.
  9. (Preferred but not necessary step): Stud the lemon peel by poking the individual cloves through the lemon peel.
  10. Place the peel into the glass, letting it hang by one of the studs on the rim of the glass.
    11. Enjoy!
