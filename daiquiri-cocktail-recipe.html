Whiskey Sip'd

Daiquiri

A delicious and sophisticated cocktail that got lost somewhere in the disco shuffle.

When made with fresh ingredients, it's the perfect cocktail:

    Ingredients
  • 2 oz Light Rum of your choice
  • .75 oz of freshly squeezed
    lime juice
  • .50 oz simple syrup (1:1)
  • Lime peel for garnish
    Tools
  • Cocktail Shaker
  • Hawthorne Strainer
  • Fine Double Strainer
  • Coupe or Cocktail Glass
  • Jigger
    Method
  1. Add all ingredients to cocktail shaker.
  2. Fill with ice.
  3. Shake vigorously until the tins frost and are cold in your hands.
  4. Using the Hawthorne strainer to hold back the ice, pour through the fine double strainer into a chilled coupe or cocktail glass.
  5. Express the oil from the lime peel into the glass and rub the peel along the full rim of the glass.
  6. Twist the lime peel and hang it on the rim of the glass.
    7. Enjoy!
