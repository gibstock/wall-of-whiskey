Welcome to the Wall of Whiskey!

Hello! My name is Andre, and I've worked at a bunch of great restaurants and bars as a bartender for over a decade. This year, I find myself working at a Whiskey bar with one of the most impressive bottle collections I've had the pleasure of working with. As a bartender, you become very familiar with a ton of product and how to sell that sweet sweet nectar to anyone. But seeing how passionate the owner was about these bottles gave me an idea:

I WILL TRY THEM ALL!

This is definitely a personal project/journey to get a better understanding of whiskey drinkers, and I figured I'd throw it up on the ol' net in case anyone was interested. I know there is a lot of spectacular Whiskey ettiquete and lingo, and I don't know any of it! So until I learn, this will be written pretty straight forward.

I don't neccessarily have a timeline on how often I'll make these posts, but I'm eager to learn so I imagine they will be quite frequent. If you've stumbled on this some how, stay tuned!

Oh yeah, the wall in the pic? Just a tiny portion of the collection. I've got some drinking to do...
