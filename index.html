Welcome to the Wall of Whiskey!

Nov 26, 2019 By Wall of Whiskey

Hello Friends!

Who am I?

I've worked at a bunch of great restaurants and bars as a bartender for over a decade.

This year I find myself working at a Whiskey bar with one of the most impressive whiskey collections I've seen.

I see. But why Male Models?

Well as a bartender, or anyone in the service industry really, you know how to sell that sweet sweet nectar to anyone. But seeing how passionate the owner was about these bottles gave me an idea:

I MUST CLIMB

THE WHISKEY WALL !

This is a personal project/journey to find a better understanding of whiskey drinkers and become a seasoned one in the process!

I'll add the bottles I taste to my own wall at the top of this page so stick around and watch my it grow!

Whisk(e)y Noob...

These reviews will be written pretty straight forward. Everyone can enjoy and talk about whiskey without prejudice because it all comes down to a very personal preference and experience.

You took a $90 sip and hated it? Valid. Your favorite is a $6 bottle? Valid.


Is it an everday thang?


I don't necessarily have a timeline on how often I'll make these posts, but I'm eager to learn so I imagine they will be quite frequent. If you've stumbled on this some how, stay tuned!
Please message me with suggestions of what you'd like to see featured!


What else will be here?

I plan on posting my favorite versions of cocktail recipes. There are so many recipes out there, these are just my faves.

I've got some drinking to do...
