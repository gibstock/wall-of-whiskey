Welcome to the Wall of Whiskey!

Nov 26, 2019 By Wall of Whiskey

Hello Friends!

I've worked at a bunch of great restaurants and bars as a bartender for over a decade.

This year, I find myself working at a Whiskey bar with one of the most impressive bottle collections I've seen.


As a bartender, or anyone in the service industry really, you know how to sell that sweet sweet nectar to anyone. But seeing how passionate the owner was about these bottles gave me an idea:

I WILL TRY

THEM ALL!

This is a personal project/journey to find a better understanding of whiskey drinkers, and figured I'd throw it up on the ol' net in case anyone was interested.

I know there is a lot of spectacular Whiskey ettiquete and lingo that I'm not fully versed in yet, so this will be written pretty straight forward until I learn.

I don't neccessarily have a timeline on how often I'll make these posts, but I'm eager to learn so I imagine they will be quite frequent. If you've stumbled on this some how, stay tuned!

Oh yeah, the wall in the pic? Just a tiny portion of the collection. I've got some drinking to do...
